Trikatuka is a simple tool helps you to move your playlists, tracks, albums and artists from one account to another.
  • If playlist is "Collaborative", it will be followed.
  • If playlist is "Public" or "Private" it will be copied.
  • Playlists followed by old user will be followed by new user.
How to:
  • Login to old account
  • Login to new account
  • In "Playlists" tab you can transfer your all playlists by clicking "Transfer All" button. You can also select some playlists and move only them by clicking "Transfer selected"
  • In "Tracks" tab you can see your saved tracks, and transfer them by clicking "Transfer all" button.
  • In "Albums" tab you can see your saved albums, and transfer them by clicking "Transfer all" button.
  • In "Artists" tab you can see your followed artists, and transfer them by clicking "Transfer all" button.
